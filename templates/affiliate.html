What 1099-DA requires

The 1099-DA reports proceeds for digital asset dispositions. For a complete crypto tax result you typically also need cost basis (what you paid) to compute gains/losses. Cost basis often isn't fully captured on the form - especially across multiple exchanges/wallets.

Why TaxClaw stops at extraction

TaxClaw is built to extract and organize your tax form fields (and export them). Cost basis reconciliation is a separate workflow that depends on exchange APIs, on-chain wallets, lot selection rules, transfers, and your full trading history. For that, you'll want a dedicated reconciliation platform.
